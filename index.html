VESPER AND THE LEGENDARY WORLDWIDE FAIRMONT HOTEL CHAIN PRESENT A NEW PROJECT UNITING A FIVE-STAR HOTEL AND DELUXE APARTMENTS. THIS EXCLUSIVE PROJECT IN THE MAIN STREET OF MOSCOW IS A GAME-CHANGER FOR LIFE AESTHETICS IN A MODERN MEGAPOLIS.

ABOUT

Two buildings, one a hotel and the other comprising private apartments, create a single ensemble. They are connected by shared floors where there are restaurant and bar, a wellness and spa area with a pool, a shopping gallery, conference halls and a parking lot. In addition, each of the buildings has a separate entrance. All the apartments are managed by the Fairmont organisation, and private owners will have access to the entire hotel infrastructure and impeccable round-the-clock service.

LOCATION

The Fairmont | Vesper Project completes the architectural ensemble of Tverskaya Street, occupying the last free area of the city’s main street - in the very heart of the cultural, business, political, fine-dining and night life area of Moscow. Residence in the very city centre creates a special pace of life. Everything that one of the largest megapolises in the world can captivate you with is within easy walking distance: philharmonic halls, theatres, the main museums, top class restaurants, boutiques and the Patriarch Ponds - one of the most fashionable and conceptualised areas of the city.

ARCHITECTURE

The architectural concept of the project, created by the SPEECH architectural bureau under the leadership of Sergey Choban, fits in organically with the elegant range of historic buildings.

The combination of bold lines with smooth contours and precise proportions echoes the monumental character and scale of Tverskaya Street.

INTERIORS

DAVID ROCKWELL

Founder and president, Rockwell Group

«For Fairmont | Vesper Hotel & Residences, we were inspired by the grandiosity of Moscow’s iconic spaces. From the hotel lobby lounge, to the restaurant, to residences, guests will encounter endless discovery, but also begin to pick up on the repetition of warmth, vibrancy, and artistry.»

The spacious lobby is decorated in noble, warm colours. The combination of dark and light marble flooring follows the geometry of the frosted glass and gold metal elements, separating the lobby and lounge area. The strict forms and the powerful rhythm of vertical lines in the decor are balanced with furniture that emphasizes its own soft, rounded silhouettes.

APARTMENTS

A special palette of colours, shapes and materials has been developed for the interior concept. The play of textures, the delicate balance of light accents and the geometry of lines in the decor and furniture hark back to the American avant-garde and eclecticism, reminiscent of the reserved luxury of the interiors of New York skyscrapers.

All the apartments have a superb quality of finish and include fully-equipped kitchens and bathrooms. The interiors are easily adapted to any interior-design solutions.

PENTHOUSES

Special attention must be reserved for the penthouse, which occupies the whole top floor and has its own patio, separate entrance and lift.

CONCIERGE SERVICE 24/7

We value your time. The concierge is your perfect assistant, who is not only ready at any time to reserve a table in any of the best restaurants or bars in the city, or secure invitations to a high-profile premiere or exhibition, but can also recommend a nutritionist, a personal trainer, a wine consultant or call a maid or nanny for your children.

NANNY SERVICE

The flawless Fairmont service is available for all family members, including even the youngest ones. Experienced and professional nannies will look after your children and arrange their time in the most rewarding and beneficial way, enabling their parents not to be distracted from their important business.

MAID SERVICES

To maintain perfect cleanliness and excellent order in the apartments, you can use the services of Fairmont maids. In particular, you can order furniture and carpet cleaning or ask them to prepare the apartments for your return from a business trip or vacation.

TAILORING SERVICES AND DRY CLEANING

To always look perfect, you do not need to spend a lot of time taking care of your wardrobe. Use the hotel services, including tailors, laundry, dry cleaning, ironing and shoe care. Your items will be carefully kept in excellent condition and delivered to your apartment by Fairmont staff.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT AND APARTMENT SERVICES

Additional services include maintenance of the apartments by the hotel technical staff. They will help you troubleshoot any issue with your apartment, and during your absence, employees will regularly check the operation of all of its systems.

DOORMAN

The apartment owners can use the doorman service to welcome guests at the building entrance.
