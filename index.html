VESPER AND THE LEGENDARY WORLDWIDE FAIRMONT HOTEL CHAIN PRESENT A NEW PROJECT UNITING A FIVE-STAR HOTEL AND DELUXE APARTMENTS. THIS EXCLUSIVE PROJECT IN THE MAIN STREET OF MOSCOW IS A GAME-CHANGER FOR LIFE AESTHETICS IN A MODERN MEGAPOLIS.

ABOUT

Two buildings, one a hotel and the other comprising private apartments, create a single ensemble. They are connected by shared floors where there are restaurant and bar, a wellness and spa area with a pool, a shopping gallery, conference halls and a parking lot. In addition, each of the buildings has a separate entrance. All the apartments are managed by the Fairmont organisation, and private owners will have access to the entire hotel infrastructure and impeccable round-the-clock service.

LOCATION

The Fairmont | Vesper Project completes the architectural ensemble of Tverskaya Street, occupying the last free area of the city‚Äôs main street - in the very heart of the cultural, business, political, fine-dining and night life area of Moscow. Residence in the very city centre creates a special pace of life. Everything that one of the largest megapolises in the world can captivate you with is within easy walking distance: philharmonic halls, theatres, the main museums, top class restaurants, boutiques and the Patriarch Ponds - one of the most fashionable and conceptualised areas of the city.

ARCHITECTURE

The architectural concept of the project, created by the SPEECH architectural bureau under the leadership of Sergey Choban, fits in organically with the elegant range of historic buildings.

The combination of bold lines with smooth contours and precise proportions echoes the monumental character and scale of Tverskaya Street.

A special palette of colours, shapes and materials has been developed for the interior concept.
